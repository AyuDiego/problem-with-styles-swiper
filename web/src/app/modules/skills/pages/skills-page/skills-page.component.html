Development and layout of the Front end of a new Nx Monorepro application that is being developed for the electoral council of the government of the Netherlands.
Here I was in charge of making new developments in the application and to develop and layout a library of custom components with PrimeNg for all the applications of the project. In addition, I developed and designed a Home portal project that would have the home page of all the projects in the repository.

Technology Stack: Nx, Angular 14.2, PrimeNg 14, SCSS, SASS, Html5 and for translations Transloco. Figma for layouts.
